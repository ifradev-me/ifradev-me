## Featured Projects / Proyek Unggulan

### 🏪 [Warkop MJD — Multi-Tenant Ordering System](https://github.com/ifradev-me/mjd-multitenant-system)
Real-time POS system for a multi-kitchen café. Customers scan a QR code, orders route to the right kitchen, and waiters receive notifications via Telegram Bot — no extra app needed.

Sistem POS real-time untuk warkop multi-dapur. Customer scan QR di meja, order di-route ke dapur yang tepat, waiter dapat notifikasi via Telegram Bot.

`Express 5` `Socket.io` `PostgreSQL` `React 19` `Telegraf` `Docker`

---

### 📊 [Konten Analitik — TikTok Diagnosis for UMKM](https://konten-analitik.pages.dev)
More than a dashboard — it explains *why* a video performs well or poorly and *what to do next*, in plain language any business owner can understand. Built for Indonesian small businesses.

Lebih dari sekadar dashboard — menjelaskan *kenapa* video performa baik/buruk dan *apa yang harus dilakukan*, dalam bahasa yang dipahami pemilik usaha.

`React 19` `Vite` `Tailwind v4` `Recharts` `OpenRouter AI` `Cloudflare Pages`

---

### 🌿 [NutriSight — AI Nutrition Tracker](https://github.com/nutrisight-innovilage/nutrisight_apps)
Mobile app: snap a photo of your meal → AI calculates calories, protein, carbs, fat. Offline-first with a custom sync engine. Includes a 45+ Indonesian ingredient database built from actual recipes.

Aplikasi mobile: foto makanan → AI hitung nutrisi. Offline-first dengan sync engine custom. Database 45+ bahan makanan Indonesia dari resep nyata.

`React Native` `Expo` `Appwrite` `OpenRouter AI` `NativeWind` `Strategy Pattern`

---

### 🤖 [Kroeng USK — Robotics Community Portal](https://kroeng.netlify.app)
Full community hub: member registration system, knowledge base, achievements, admin CMS, and SEO-optimized Next.js architecture. Built for USK's robotics community.

Portal komunitas lengkap: sistem pendaftaran anggota, knowledge base, galeri prestasi, admin CMS, dan arsitektur Next.js SEO-optimized untuk komunitas robotika USK.

`Next.js 13` `Supabase` `shadcn/ui` `TypeScript` `Netlify`

---

### 📁 [WhatsApp to Web — Easy File Transfer](https://github.com/ifradev-me/whatsapp-to-web-Easy-transfer)
Send a photo or PDF to a WhatsApp bot → get a download link. Auto-compressed, password-protected, and auto-deleted after your chosen retention period. No account needed.

Kirim foto/PDF ke bot WhatsApp → dapat link download. Otomatis dikompres, bisa diproteksi password, dan auto-hapus sesuai waktu yang dipilih. Tanpa perlu akun.

`Express` `TypeScript` `WhatsApp Baileys` `Next.js` `Appwrite` `Docker`

---

### 🌐 [Culture-X — Aceh Cultural Literacy Platform](https://culture-x.netlify.app)
Social platform for educating Gen Z about Acehnese heritage — AI chatbot tutor, short-form video (TikTok-style), discussion forum, marketplace, and friend system.

Platform sosial edukasi budaya Aceh untuk generasi muda — chatbot AI literasi budaya, video pendek, forum diskusi, marketplace produk budaya, dan sistem pertemanan.

`React 18` `Vite` `Appwrite` `OpenAI GPT-4o` `Framer Motion`
